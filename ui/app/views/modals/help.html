You can reach out to Vanguard@marklogic.com with any question regarding Demo-Cat. A number of frequently asked questions are listed below:

Q: What credentials do I use to login? Do my old Demo-Cat credentials work?
A: Old Demo-Cat credentials will no longer work. The new Demo-Cat authenticates against the MarkLogic LDAP, which means everyone should be able to login with their MarkLogic Domain credentials.
Q: What do I do if my credentials don’t work?
A: Contact Vanguard@marklogic.com
Q: What are the different roles and permissions?
A: We have 3 unique roles:
  • Admin - for Vanguard only.
  • Write - for anyone in Sales Engineering (SE).
  • Read - for everyone else (not in Vanguard or SE).
Please contact Vanguard@marklogic.com if you have questions or feel that you don't have the correct permissions.
Q: After I login, how can I see all of the demos?
A: Click the “Show all demos” button to get a search all view of the demos. You can also conduct a specific search by entering the title of a demo.
Q: How do I request server space to build a demo?
A: Demos with limited amounts of data can be deployed on one of the Vanguard demo servers. You can contact Vanguard@marklogic.com about this.
Q: How do I add an existing demo to DC?
A: You need admin or write permissions:
  • If you have one of these permissions, you will see a “Create” button in the header bar of Demo-Cat.
  • Click the “Create” button and fill in the required fields and as many optional fields as possible.
  • Click “Save” at the end of the page.
If you do not have Admin or Write permissions, please contact Vanguard@marklogic.com and we will add it for you.
Q: What does “Follow this demo” do?
A: It will apply alerting to changes to that particular demo:
  • You will be notified via email every time the demo is edited.
  • Make sure you have edited your personal profile through the Account link on the top-right of Demo-Cat, and have added a valid email address.
  • Make sure to click the (+) button next to the email address field to append it to the list of emails (you are allowed to enter multiple email addresses).
  • Make sure to click “Submit” to persist your profile changes.
Q: How do I de-select facets to go back to the original search results?
A: Click the trash icon next to each facet and you will get back to the original search results.
Q: What is the small “i” icon when I add a demo?
A: If you hover over the “i” icon, it will give you a pop-up describing the field.
Q: When I’m adding a new demo, how do I use the (+) sign? (i.e. When adding Features)
A: After typing your entry in the field, click the (+) sign to add it to the list.
Q: Why am I getting an error message when trying to upload a “Demo Attachment”?
A: The upload size of the attachment is capped at 5MB per file. If you have an attachment larger than 5MB, please contact Vanguard@marklogic.com for assistance.
Q: When I add an entry for “Open Bug or Enhancements”, where does it go?
A: All entries are recorded inside the Demo-Cat database. That includes demo details, but also bugs, RFEs, comments, memos, links to media, and any attachment that was uploaded.
Q: What does “Access Restricted” mean?
A: When a demo or the data associated with the demo cannot be shown to the public. Please read the “Restriction Details” for more information.
